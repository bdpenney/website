Tourist's Guide to Greece

Welcome to the tourist's guide to Greece! Look no further for best spots and dishes to try while you're in this European paradise!

Must See Locations

Acropolis

This beautiful historical location is home to an ancient citadel lovations which is built on a rocky outcop of the city of Athens

Santorini

This island is home to the most historical and common conceptions of Greece with their beautiful white with blue trim houses and beautiful beaches

Common Greek Dishes To Eat

Souvlaki

Souvlaki is a meat and vegetable meal that is grilled on a skewer

Moussaka

This traditional dish is an eggplant based dish often containing ground beef done in a tomato based sauce

Baklava

Baklava is a pastry dish that contains filo filled with chopped nuts and is fused together with syrup or honey
